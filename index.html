About Us

The most essential and fundamental aspect of culture is the study of literature because the culture of a civilization is its art and literature through which it rises to consciousness of itself and defines vision of the world. Literature is one of the most interesting and significant expression of humanity as it is the language of the soul, and the conduit to bind cultures and civilizations together. Celebrating this versatility and appreciating the indelible power of language in all its moods and genres Blue Bells Model School Sec-4 Gurugram is hosting

LA FÊTE DE LA LANGUE’ -A FESTIVAL OF LANGUAGES -CHAPTER IV 2022 on November 25 & 26, 2022 under the aegis of GPSC.

The event is our sincere effort to make the young learners broaden their perspective, enhance the gamut of their experiences and redefine their artistic, critical ,analytical and creative prowess through a plethora of interesting events.
